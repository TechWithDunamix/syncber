Welcome to the **IAM & PAM Professional** course — your complete path to mastering enterprise identity security. Over six comprehensive modules, you'll go from foundational IAM concepts to advanced PAM architecture and deployment strategies.

<Aside variant="info" title="Course Format">
  Self-paced · 6 modules · Estimated 40 hours · Hands-on exercises and real-world scenarios included.
</Aside>

---

## What You'll Learn

<Steps>
1.  **IAM Fundamentals** — Identity lifecycles, directory services (AD, LDAP, Cloud), user provisioning models (JIT, SCIM, HR sync)
2.  **Authentication & Authorization** — MFA, SSO, federated identity, OAuth 2.0, OIDC, SAML, RBAC, ABAC, policy engines
3.  **Privileged Access Management** — Credential vaulting, session management, just-in-time access, least privilege, privileged account discovery
4.  **Identity Governance** — Role mining and design, access certifications, Segregation of Duties (SoD), entitlement reviews
5.  **Compliance & Auditing** — SOX, GDPR, PCI DSS, HIPAA, audit trail design, compliance automation, breach notification
6.  **Architecture & Deployment** — Hub-and-spoke and cloud-hybrid patterns, HA/DR, integrations (HR, ITSM, SIEM, DevOps), operational best practices
</Steps>
