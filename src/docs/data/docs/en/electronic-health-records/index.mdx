Welcome to **Electronic Health Records Management** — your complete guide to understanding, implementing, and managing electronic health record systems in modern healthcare.

Major transformations in health information technology (IT) have given us access to various data and services at our fingertips. With health IT, providers can now reach out to patients, hospitals, pharmacies, laboratories, and other organizations that make up the circle of care. At the heart of this transformation is the **Electronic Health Record (EHR)** — a digital version of paper medical charts that has fundamentally changed how healthcare is documented, shared, and improved.

<Aside variant="info" title="Course Format">
  Self-paced · 4 phases · 11 modules · Real-world case studies, practical examples, and hands-on exercises included.
</Aside>

## What You'll Learn

<Steps>
1. **Phase 1: EHR Foundations** — What is an EHR, EHR vs EMR, contents of the record, documentation importance
2. **Phase 2: Transition & Processes** — Paper-based procedures, the transition to electronic systems, EHR contributors and ownership
3. **Phase 3: EHR Software & Systems** — EHR software capabilities, Clinical Decision Support (CDS), Practice Management Software (PMS)
4. **Phase 4: Impact & Outcomes** — Advantages and disadvantages, improved patient care, disaster response, documentation quality
</Steps>

## Course Structure

| Phase | Modules | Topics Covered |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| **1. EHR Foundations** | Introduction to EHR, Contents of the EHR, Documentation Importance | Purpose of EHR, EMR vs EHR, patient care impact, administrative/clinical/legal content, documentation standards, reimbursement, liability, value-based care |
| **2. Transition & Processes** | Paper-Based Procedures, Transition to EHR, Contributors & Ownership | Appointment books, progress notes, superbills, CMS-1500, Meaningful Use, CPOE, Recovery Act, financial incentives, care contributors, patient ownership, HIPAA, professional discretion |
| **3. EHR Software & Systems** | EHR Software & CDS, Practice Management Software | Software capabilities, patient portals, e-prescribing, clinical decision support, evidence-based guidelines, PMS features, patient demographics, appointment scheduling, accounting procedures |
| **4. Impact & Outcomes** | Advantages & Impact, Disaster Response & Documentation | Quality of care, patient participation, care coordination, cost savings, disaster resilience, documentation improvement, error reduction, public health outcomes |
